HIMACHAL PRADESH

Snow-kissed valleys, alpine lakes, and pine-scented air. From Shimla's heritage charm to Spiti's lunar quiet — Himachal is for slow travellers, sharp horizons, and starlit nights.

Best Time To Visit

March to June for valleys. October to February for snow.
