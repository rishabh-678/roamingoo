Eastern Himalayas · Kanchenjunga's Watch

SIKKIM

Monasteries on cliffs, lakes that change colour with the sky, and the third-highest peak on earth watching every move. Sikkim is small in size and impossibly large in feeling.

Best Time To Visit

March to May for rhododendrons. October to December for clearest skies.
