WEST BENGAL

From the misty tea slopes of Darjeeling to the mangrove maze of the Sundarbans, with Kolkata's tram-clatter heritage in between — Bengal is half a continent in one state.

Best Time To Visit

October to March — clearest skies for hills, coolest for the delta.
