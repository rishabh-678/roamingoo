About The Tour

A slow, well-paced loop through Himachal's most loved towns. You'll start in Manali with a day to acclimatise, climb up to Solang Valley for snow play, and end with a quiet night above Old Manali where the river never stops.

Everything is arranged for you — private cab, hand-picked stays, breakfasts and one local dinner each day. We leave a couple of half-days completely free so you can wander, write, sleep, or chase that one café you read about on the way up.

  • · Private vehicle with experienced mountain driver
  • · English-speaking local guide on full-day excursions
  • · 24/7 trip concierge on WhatsApp
  • · Free cancellation up to 30 days before departure
Day 1 — 2

Old Manali

Arrival, riverside walks, fairy-lit cafés, and a slow acclimatisation evening.

Day 3 — 4

Solang & Rohtang

Snow slopes, gondola ride, and (weather permitting) a day-trip to Rohtang Pass.

Day 5 — 6

Naggar & Return

Heritage castle, apple orchards, and a long lunch before the drive back to Chandigarh.
