UTTARAKHAND

Sacred peaks, Char Dham temples, and the Ganga in its purest form. From Rishikesh's yoga decks to the Valley of Flowers — Uttarakhand is for seekers and sky-watchers alike.

Best Time To Visit

April to June for valleys. September to November for clearest skies.

Getting There
